About MRIIOT

Pioneering Industrial Digital Transformation Since 2016

Our Story

Founded in 2016 in Chicago, MRIIOT (Mr. IIoT) emerged from a simple observation: manufacturing companies needed a bridge between their operational technology and the promise of Industry 4.0. We saw brilliant engineers struggling with complex integrations, months-long deployments, and consultants who didn't understand the factory floor.

We believed there had to be a better way. A way that respected the knowledge of plant engineers while leveraging the power of modern AI. A way that could deploy in hours, not months. A way that put industrial intelligence in the hands of the people who need it most.

That belief became DIME - our flagship platform that transforms how manufacturers connect, understand, and optimize their operations.

◎

Our Mission

Democratize industrial intelligence by making advanced data integration and AI accessible to every manufacturer, regardless of size or technical expertise.

Our Vision

A world where every factory floor decision is informed by real-time intelligence, where machines communicate seamlessly, and where continuous improvement is automated.

What Sets Us Apart

Factory Floor First

Speed of Implementation
