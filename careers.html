Senior Industrial Protocol Engineer

Remote / Chicago, IL

Lead the expansion of DIME's industrial connectivity layer. You'll architect high-performance protocol implementations that connect to the world's factories, handling everything from legacy PLCs to cutting-edge IIoT devices.

What You'll Do

  • Design and implement native connectors for industrial protocols (OPC-UA, Modbus, Ethernet/IP, MTConnect, BACnet)
  • Optimize real-time data collection for microsecond-level performance using lock-free data structures
  • Build protocol analyzers and diagnostic tools for field troubleshooting
  • Create AI-powered protocol discovery and auto-configuration systems
  • Collaborate with customers to integrate exotic and proprietary protocols

What We're Looking For

  • 5+ years implementing industrial protocols in production environments
  • Deep understanding of real-time systems and high-performance networking
  • Experience with Wireshark, protocol reverse engineering, and bit-level manipulation
  • Fluency in C#/.NET, with C/C++ and Rust experience valued
  • Battle scars from factory floors and appreciation for 24/7 reliability

Nice to Have

  • Contributions to open-source protocol libraries
  • Experience with time-sensitive networking (TSN) and industrial Ethernet
  • Knowledge of safety protocols (CIP Safety, PROFIsafe)

AI/ML Platform Engineer - Neuromorphic Systems

Remote / Chicago, IL

Pioneer the integration of neuromorphic computing into industrial applications. You'll evolve our HTM anomaly detection engine and build bridges between LLMs and real-time industrial data streams.

What You'll Do

  • Design hybrid architectures combining HTM's continuous learning with LLM reasoning
  • Build explainable AI interfaces that maintenance teams actually trust
  • Optimize sparse distributed representations for edge deployment
  • Create industrial-specific LLM fine-tuning pipelines and prompt engineering frameworks

What We're Looking For

  • 3+ years in ML engineering with focus on temporal/streaming data
  • Understanding of neuromorphic computing principles and spiking neural networks
  • Experience with online learning algorithms and concept drift
  • Proficiency in Python, PyTorch/TensorFlow, and C# for production systems
  • Passion for making AI accessible to non-data scientists

Nice to Have

  • Contributions to Numenta's HTM research or similar projects
  • Experience with industrial time-series analysis
  • Background in neuroscience or cognitive computing
  • Edge AI deployment experience

Senior Platform Engineer - Real-Time Systems

Remote / Chicago, IL

Architect the backbone of industrial intelligence. You'll evolve our Disruptor-based message passing system, build fault-tolerant data pipelines, and ensure DIME scales from edge devices to cloud deployments.

What You'll Do

  • Evolve our lock-free ring buffer architecture for 100K+ messages/second throughput
  • Design distributed system components for high availability and horizontal scaling
  • Implement intelligent data routing with content-based filtering and transformation
  • Build observability into every layer - because you can't optimize what you can't measure
  • Create developer-friendly APIs that hide complexity without sacrificing power

What We're Looking For

  • 5+ years building high-performance distributed systems
  • Deep understanding of concurrent programming, lock-free algorithms, and CPU cache optimization
  • Experience with message queuing, event streaming, and real-time data processing
  • Strong C#/.NET skills with appreciation for systems programming
  • Track record of building developer tools and platforms others love to use

Nice to Have

  • Contributions to LMAX Disruptor or similar high-performance libraries
  • Experience with time-series databases and columnar storage
  • Knowledge of industrial middleware (DDS, MQTT, AMQP)
  • Kubernetes operator development experience

Industrial Customer Success Engineer

Remote / Travel Required

Be the bridge between cutting-edge technology and real-world factories. You'll help manufacturers deploy DIME in hours, not months, and turn their data into competitive advantage.

What You'll Do

  • Translate between IT and OT teams, building bridges across organizational silos
