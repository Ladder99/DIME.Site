## Key Differences from Platform Page

1. **Business Focus**: ROI, cost savings, and business metrics vs technical architecture
2. **Problem-First**: Each feature addresses a specific pain point
3. **Pricing Transparency**: Clear tiers and what's included
4. **Industry Solutions**: Tailored to specific verticals
5. **Competitive Positioning**: Direct comparisons with alternatives
6. **Success Metrics**: Real customer results and case studies
7. **Interactive Tools**: ROI calculator for immediate value demonstration

This approach makes the Features page a sales tool while the Platform page remains the technical reference.
