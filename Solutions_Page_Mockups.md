## Implementation Notes

1. **Interactive Elements**
   - Industry cards expand to show detailed solutions
   - Solution builder provides real-time configuration
   - Download templates without registration for quick evaluation
   - Case studies include actual screenshots and data

2. **Content Strategy**
   - Lead with industry-specific pain points
   - Show compliance certifications prominently
   - Include equipment/system logos for familiarity
   - Provide calculators for industry-specific metrics

3. **Conversion Points**
   - "Build Your Solution" interactive tool
   - Industry-specific ROI calculators
   - Free template downloads
   - Pilot program offers

4. **Differentiation**
   - Pre-built templates vs custom development
   - Industry expert knowledge built-in
   - Compliance documentation included
   - Peer benchmarking data
